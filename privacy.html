Privacy Policy

We do not store any email content. Our system processes email content transiently to provide AI-assisted features and does not persist message bodies.

GDPR

We act as a data processor. We process personal data only for the purpose of delivering the service. Users may exercise data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection) by contacting support.

CCPA

We do not sell personal information. California residents may request disclosure or deletion of personal information collected about them by contacting support.

Data Security

We apply least-privilege OAuth scopes (gmail.readonly, gmail.modify) and encrypt data in transit. Email content is not stored in our systems.
