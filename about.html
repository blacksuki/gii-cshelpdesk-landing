Our Mission

For most e-commerce businesses, customer support representatives spend up to 40% of their working hours switching tabs between their email inbox and Shopify dashboards, manually hunting down shipping tracking IDs, customer orders, and refund policies.

giiHelpdesk was founded to bridge this gap. We empower merchants by integrating context-aware, privacy-preserving AI assistance directly inside their daily tool — Gmail. By fetching order metadata securely and constructing draft responses instantly, we turn support from a tedious chore into a seamless operation.

Core Brand Values

Our design philosophy prioritizes three unbreakable technical boundaries that define our software suite:

⚡

10x Support Speed

By eliminating page reloads and manual copy-pasting, we reduce standard response compilation times from minutes to under 5 seconds.

🔒

Zero-Content Storage

We never store, log, or persist merchant emails or private order data. All requests are processed securely in volatile in-memory states.

💡

No Training Required

Our UI is designed for immediate intuition. New hires and interns can act as experienced support representatives from day one.

Our Story

Founded in 2026 by a collective of e-commerce veterans and AI software engineers, we saw how small to medium Shopify merchants struggled to keep up with large enterprise support operations. The existing Help Desk solutions were heavy, complicated, and forced merchants to pay massive subscription fees for features they never touched.

We designed giiHelpdesk as a clean, lightweight alternative: a dedicated, specialized Gmail Add-on that directly talks to Shopify APIs, leveraging state-of-the-art LLMs to draft flawless answers. Today, we are proud to serve Shopify stores globally, helping support teams work happier and faster.
