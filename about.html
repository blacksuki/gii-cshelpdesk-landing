About giiHelpdeskAgent

We build AI-powered agent for Shopify merchants, focusing on speed, privacy, and accuracy. Our agent keeps your workflow inside the inbox while leveraging Shopify data and modern LLMs.

giiHelpdesk agent is a easy using tool which can help your customer support to work more efficiently and no more training, so even intern can work as a better experienced customer support.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What permissions are required?

We request minimal permissions: gmail.readonly to analyze the current email and gmail.modify to insert drafts. We never store your email content.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We have a zero-content-storage design. Your email data is processed in memory and never saved.

Which Shopify plans are supported?

All Shopify plans are supported.

Can I use this with my Google Workspace (GSuite) account?

Yes, it works with both regular Gmail accounts and Google Workspace accounts with custom domains.
