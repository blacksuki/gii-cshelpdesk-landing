What's next

These are the specific improvements we're working on now. Priorities can shift based on what early users tell us - if something here is critical for you, email us and we'll move it up.

Building now

Multi-rule matching per email

Currently, the system matches one SOP rule per email. Some complaints trigger multiple rules (e.g., an order that is both unfulfilled AND past the refund window). We're building rule priority stacking so the system handles these edge cases correctly.

Why it matters: Eliminates the "no rule matched" fallback on complex cases, which currently pushes the decision entirely to the agent.
Building now

Custom SOP rule editor (self-serve)

Right now, adding custom rules requires contacting us. We're building a self-serve UI inside the dashboard where you can define, test, and activate your own refund and cancellation rules without engineering help.

Why it matters: Your refund policy changes. Your rules should too - without waiting on us.
Planned

Audit log export (CSV)

Monthly export of your action audit log to CSV. Useful for finance reconciliation, CS manager reviews, or dispute resolution with payment processors.

Why it matters: Chargebacks sometimes come 60â€“90 days after the original complaint. You need a trail that survives that window.
Planned

Shopify tagged order notes

When an agent confirms a CS action, automatically write a structured note to the Shopify order (not just a cancellation - a timestamped note with rule name, agent ID, and action taken). Searchable from Shopify admin without opening our dashboard.

Why it matters: Your ops team and 3PL can see CS actions directly in Shopify without needing access to giiHelpdeskAgent.
Planned

Weekly digest email to team managers

Automated weekly email to the account owner: number of complaints processed, rule match rate, escalations flagged, actions taken per agent. No login required to see if the system is working.

Why it matters: Managers shouldn't need to log into a dashboard to know if CS is running smoothly.
Considering

Shopify Inbox integration

Extend SOP rule matching to Shopify Inbox chat threads. Same rule engine, different input channel. Would let teams using Shopify's built-in chat apply the same policy enforcement without switching to Gmail.

Why it matters: Some DTC teams use Shopify Inbox as their primary CS channel. We'd reach them without requiring a workflow change.

What we're explicitly not building

Fully autonomous refund processing (no human confirm), abandoned cart recovery, social media DM monitoring, or a native mobile app. These aren't on our roadmap because they either conflict with our human-in-the-loop principle or are outside our core use case (Gmail-based complaint handling for Shopify DTC).
