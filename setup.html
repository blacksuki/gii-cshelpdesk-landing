Gmail Add-on Setup & Shopify Integration

Follow this comprehensive setup guide to connect your Shopify store and configure the AI assistant within your Gmail inbox.

1

Acquire the Add-on

Get the certified giiHelpDeskAgent add-on directly from the official Google Workspace Marketplace. Click the link below to open our marketplace listing page.

Ready to integrate? Install from the Store:
Install from Google Workspace Marketplace
2

Grant Scopes & Permissions

During installation, Google will prompt you to grant the necessary permissions. These scopes are strictly used to read order details and insert response drafts. Below is why each scope is required:

OAuth Scope Why It is Essential
gmail.addons.current.message.readonly Allows the AI to read the selected customer email to analyze their intent (e.g., WISMO, refund).
script.external_request Enables the add-on to query your Shopify API and retrieve order and shipping info.
gmail.readonly Allows the AI to inspect email thread history so the generated reply matches previous context.
gmail.compose Enables the add-on to construct draft replies in Gmail so you can review and edit before sending.
3

Open the Add-on Sidebar

Once installed, open Gmail in your desktop web browser. Open any customer email conversation thread, and look at the right utility panel. You will see our green giiHelpdesk logo icon.

Click this icon to expand the side panel. If your Shopify store is not connected yet, the add-on will guide you to complete the store authentication.

💡 Gmail Interface Guide

Open any email thread inside Gmail and find the g icon in the right-side vertical sidebar.

A

Method 1: Connect via Gmail Sidebar (Recommended)

You can authorize connection instantly when opening the add-on side panel in Gmail for the first time:

  1. Open the Gmail Add-on sidebar (as described in the setup steps).
  2. In the text field inside the Add-on panel, enter your Shopify permanent store domain (e.g. your-shop.myshopify.com).
  3. Click the Authorize Connection button.
  4. A secure Shopify OAuth permission page will open in a browser overlay window. If prompted, log in to your Shopify account.
  5. Click Install App to authorize the connection. The popup window will close automatically.
  6. The Add-on will auto-refresh and display ✓ Connected!
B

Method 2: Connect via Portal Dashboard

Alternatively, merchants can configure store mappings beforehand using the centralized web control panel:

  1. Sign in to your merchant account at giihelpdesk.com Portal.
  2. On the main dashboard, scroll down to find the **Shopify Integration** card.
  3. Click **Set Up Shopify Domain** / **Update Domain** to enter your store address.
  4. Click **Verify Connection**. A secure Shopify installation window will open.
  5. Review the requested scope list in Shopify Admin, then click **Install App**.
  6. Once successful, you will be redirected back with a success toast notification.

🛡️ Secure Shopify OAuth
